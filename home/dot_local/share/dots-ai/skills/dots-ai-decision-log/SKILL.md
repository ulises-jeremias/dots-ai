# Decision Log (WHAT)

Use for lightweight decisions made in meetings or project discussions.

## Choose the right artifact

- Use **`dots-ai-decision-log`** for lightweight operational, product, or project decisions.
- Use **`dots-ai-agreement`** for explicit commitments or terms among parties.
- Use **`dots-ai-adr`** for durable architecture or technical decisions.

## Default guardrails

1. Apply **`dots-ai-output-handshake`** before final output.
2. Link related work items and meeting minutes where possible.
